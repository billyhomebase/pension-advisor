Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required to make this digital service work for you. They will also store information relating to regulatory questions.

Targeting cookies

Ever wondered why you suddenly start seeing ads for shoes after searching for a new pair online? This is down to targeting cookies. They track your browsing habits to ensure you see more relevant advertising across different websites. This allows us and trusted third parties build a better profile of you, to serve you with tailored ads which you might be interested in. Targeting cookies can include cookies set by social media platforms. For our full list of cookies set by trusted third parties, visit our cookie policy

Performance & experience cookies

These cookies help us better understand how you interact with our website. They let us know things like which pages are most popular, how long you visit for, and what you click on. Theyre designed to help us improve the content you see, and how easily you can get to the information you need. In a nutshell, they help us help you.

These cookies enable the website to test new website designs and provide personalised experiences based on user browsing activity.
